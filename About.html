About Us

Photun is a founder-led platform created with the goal of transforming the animation industry. Our mission is simple: to allow animation studios to reduce their workload whilst retaining the quality of the produced visuals. Recognizing the many challenges that studios face when animating a still visual, we aim to simplify the process that animation studios face, streamlining animation whilst ensuring cost-effectiveness.


Our innovative platform makes use of artificial intelligence in order to convert computer-generated imagery (CGI) into traditional 2D animation. In doing so, we aim to make animation less labor-intensive, as we understand that shorts requiring an abundance of technical skill are significantly easier to create in CGI.
